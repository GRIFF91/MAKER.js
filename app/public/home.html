Welcome to Maker.js

Maker.js is a space for makers to post a project and view work posted by others. Maker.js makes it easy for you to post projects, create a maker profile, and keep track of current and unfinished projects. You will have the ability to network with other makers and see projects others are working on.

What to do, What to do?

This is a site for you to be creative and show off your "Maker Mussels". Do you want to make a stapler cast in a jello mold? How about your very own arcade cabinet? Maybe a replica prop from your favorite movie? Your imagination is your only barrier to what you can Make!

So quit reading and Start Making!!
